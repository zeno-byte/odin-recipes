LASAGNA

DESCRIPTION

Today we are introducing you to the Keto lasagna, it taste the same as a typical lasagna but it can be consumed within a low carb diet.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  1. Start by preparing your lasagna noodles. In a microwave-safe bowl, add your cream cheese, mozzarella, and parmesan.
  2. microwave in 30 second spurts until the mozzarella has mostly melted.
  3. Whisk in the eggs until a thick and creamy batter remains.
  4. Transfer the mixture onto a lined baking sheet and spread out into a single layer.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes, until it just begins to get golden around the sides.
  6. Heat some oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot, add the onions and garlic and saute for 5 minutes , until fragrant.
  7. Add the ground beef substitute for 2-3 minutes, before adding the marinara sauce.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste and continue pan frying for a further 5 minutes. Remove from the heat.
    9. Add salt and pepper to taste and continue pan frying for a further 5 minutes. Remove from the heat.
  9. Start putting the lasagna together. In an 8 x 8-inch pan, add tin foil and allow half an inch overhanging from each side. Slice the lasagna sheet into 6 even pieces.
  10. Place two pieces on the bottom of the pan, followed by a third of the ricotta, a third of the vegetarian bolognese sauce, and a third of the mozzarella.
  11. Repeat to create two more layers. Bake in the oven for 30-35 minutes, until golden brown on top.
